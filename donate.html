    How Your Donation Helps    

What Your Money is Going Towards 

Your donation will contribute to Los Angeles river cleanup efforts. Currently the river is highly polluted and filled with various debris and trash. Donations will allow us to produce quality goods to raise awareness for the state of the Los Angeles river. A portion of your donation will also go directly into cleaning up the river.

How Donations Have Helped Us in the Past 

Money from past donations have helped us fund for our Fall/Winter homeless fund collection. These proved crutual, they helped us purchase food, water, hygene products, and other means of support for our local homeless shelter.
