    How Your Donation Helps    

What Your Money is Going Towards 

Your donation will contribute to Los Angeles River cleanup efforts as well as future Resolve Los Angeles initiatives. The river is polluted and littered with debris and trash. Your donation will enable us to contribute to cleanup efforts and raise awareness for the state of the Los Angeles River.

Our team giving funds from clothing sales and donations to Union Station homeless shelter in Los Angeles County.

Money from past donations helped us fund our Fall/Winter homeless collection. These proved crutial. They helped us purchase food, water, hygiene products, and other critical supplies for our local homeless shelter.

Resolve Los Angeles volunteers helping at Friends of the LA River's (FoLAR) Earth Day cleanup event.

Recurring or One-Time Donation? 

Either one works! You have the choice of making a one-time donation or a recurring donation which would benefit our progress with stable funding, allowing us to focus more on the quality of what we produce. A one-time donation is also appreciated. Any donation amount is greatly appreciated and vital to our effort.

Finances 

Resolve Los Angeles is a 501(c)3 nonprofit corporation and is in the process of becoming a tax-exempt organization. You can read more about our legal documentation and paperwork at:
