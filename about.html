COLLECTION




"  Our mission is to protest for and support initiatives addressing homelessness, gentrification, social injustice, and environmental issues specific to Los Angeles, through the use of streetwear."



 Our Purpose 

Resolve Los Angeles utilizes streetwear to raise awareness of (and funding for) various causes within Los Angeles County. Collections are specific to these causes and donations support Resolve LA volunteers with their efforts to ameliorate these causes. Efforts can take the form of volunteer hours (like giving out food and supplies to homeless people or physically doing cleanup activities for the LA River) or monetary donations. The means of the donations matches the particular cause. Resolve Los Angeles uses seasons, specified by a start and end date, for clothing releases.

 Our Goal 

Our goal is to spread positive messages of hope and acceptance while funding relief efforts targeted at relieving homelessness and social injustice. By wearing Resolve Los Angeles streetwear, supporters demonstrate unity and show their intention to help make our world and county a better place for all.

 Our Story 

Los Angeles is a land of opportunity, glory, dead ends, and inequality. Our backyard is a city where the poor are born into their poverty, and remain in their poverty. Resolve Los Angeles was formed knowing these issues may not be solved with charity alone, but with the hope that we can make a difference for as many people as we can reach.
