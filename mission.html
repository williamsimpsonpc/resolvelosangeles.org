Our Purpose 



Resolve Los Angeles is a non-profit online E-commerce clothing brand with the goal of helping communities by donating profits to foundations, giving out food/water and other means of assistance. Our business utilizes “seasons” for clothing releases. Each season's beginning and ending are specified by a date.

Our Goal  



Our goal is to spread positive messages of hope and acceptance while funding relief efforts targeted at relieving homelessness and social injustice. By wearing Resolve Los Angeles streetwear, supporters demonstrate unity. The proceeds from our first "season" will be given out to homeless people in the form of food, water and clothing.
